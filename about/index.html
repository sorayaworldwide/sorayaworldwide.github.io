A little about me

I'm a data-driven professional with an extensive background in higher education. Throughout my career I've worked mostly in international education, managing education abroad opportunities for students in locations all over the world. I've engaged in risk management, crisis management, developing training, assessment and evaluation, and diversity and inclusion work within my capacity as an international educator.

In addition, data has been a particular interest and focus of my work. By analyzing and visualizing data, I seek to gain insights into problems and work on solutions backed by relevant findings.
